Comparing both, the Vivo T4’s processor is slightly more powerful than the Oppo K13's, which means we can expect better multitasking and higher frame rates in games. It was branded on <a href="https://www.vivo.com/in/products/t4-5g" rel="sponsored" target="_blank">Vivo website</a> that it can decrease the power consumption by ~12%, which is a good feature. The Oppo K13’s Snapdragon 6 Gen 4 is good enough for smooth performance in daily use and gaming. This also has a cooling support and IP65 dust and water resistance rating as specified in <a href="https://www.oppo.com/in/product/oppo-k13-5g.P.P1110064" target="_blank">Oppo website</a>. If battery is your concern, no other smartphone offers a 7000+ mAh battery at this budget currently in the Indian market. So both these latest smartphones are a good deal at their prices.

Vivo T4 has some features exclusively for an immersive gaming experience, like 4d game vibration, ultra game mode to switch panels from the side bar instantly, AI Game voice changer. Both these latest smartphones are released with the tech that is prominent and promising in 2025.

Coming to the verdict of the best of the above latest smartphones, one can go for the Vivo T4 5G if the budget is up to 25,000, else Oppo K13 5G will be a good option to buy for the features at the price they are offering.

These latest smartphones come packed with exclusive features and are budget-friendly.
